Tired of scrubbing the shower doors and tile grout all the time? But can't stand living with a dirty bathroom? Don't want to use harsh and harmful chemicals? Don't want to hire a cleaning service but not that greatat removing every spot? Why not have a trained robot clean up for you 100% perfectly, 100% of the time? Program the robot's path and scrub mode once, and after that it will perform the cleaning exactly the same every time. Or, turn on learning mode; the robot will do its best to figure out a cleaning plan for your bathroom, and getting better at it every time.

Robots take care of repetitive tasks from assembling cars, to transporting goods in a warehouse to be shipped, to cleaning the windows on skyscrapers. Floor cleaning robots for the home are available, but don't yet have the capability of cleaning tile grout, climbing up walls, or cleaning windows. Our robot will do all the hard back breaking work of scrubbing all the nooks and crannies you can't seem to reach. It scrubs with your favorite natural cleaning solutions such as baking soda, vinegar, or soap. Afterwards all you need to do is spray everything down and give your robot a shower to rinse off the grime. It's also a fun addition to your home: dancing motions, melodic indicator sounds, and adorable looks!

FAQ

What if the robot runs out of power?
Cleaning bot will return to its charging base home when it runs out of power.
What stage is development at?
Cleaning bot is currently in early design stages.
